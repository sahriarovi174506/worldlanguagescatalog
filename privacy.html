Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the World Languages Catalog (WLC). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you visit the WLC website.

2. Information We Collect

As a public database, we do not require user registration. We collect minimal personal data. However, we do use industry-standard tools like Google Analytics to track generalized visitor metrics (e.g., page views, country of origin, browser type). This data is anonymized and aggregated.

3. Use of Cookies

Our website uses cookies exclusively for functionality (e.g., saving your dark mode preference) and analytics. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes. You may control cookie preferences through your web browser settings.

4. Third-Party Links

Our catalog includes links to third-party databases, such as Wikidata and Ethnologue. Once you leave our site, our privacy policy no longer applies. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

5. Contact Information

To ask questions or comment about this privacy policy and our privacy practices, contact us at:
privacy@worldlanguagescatalog.org
