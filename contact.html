How to Submit a Data Correction

  1. Identify the page URL and language ISO 639-3 code.
  2. State exactly which statement is inaccurate.
  3. Provide verifiable references (publication, date, and link).
  4. Explain whether the issue affects L1 counts, classification, script, or vitality status.

Editorial Integrity Statement

WLC prioritizes source transparency and clear distinction between first-language (L1) and second-language (L2) estimates. Corrections are reviewed by the editorial team and reflected in page update notes when accepted.
